Business Cash Advance

What is Business Cash Advance?

  • A Merchant Cash Advance (MCA) is a flexible and convenient way to get a cash flow boost for your business.
  • Unlike a business loan, MCA does not have fixed monthly repayments, but adjusts to your card sales.
  • You can use the funding for any business purpose, such as buying inventory, paying salaries, or expanding your operations.
  • The amount of funding you can get depends on your monthly card volumes. You can borrow from £3,000 to £150,000 and more, up to 175% of your card sales.
