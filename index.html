Company Profile

At Calculations Funding Limited, we understand that business funding can be a complex and daunting process. We aim to simplify the process and take the stress out of securing funding for our customers. Our team of experts will help you find the right lender to enable you to meet your business growth ambitions.

We have a specialist panel of lenders who offer a wide range of finance options. This means we can secure the right finance to match your individual business needs.

Our team of specialists has extensive experience in the commercial finance sector, which allows them to understand your specific business requirements. They have expertise in a wide variety of commercial finance, meaning they can provide expert advice on which product is the right fit for your business.

Our services

We have a wide range of finance products, with limits from as low as £3,000 up to £50 million, which means we’re confident of finding the perfect fit to help you grow your business.

Business Cash Advance

A unique cash advance option that allows you to repay the borrowed amount only when you receive your payment.

Unsecured Business Loans

Get quick, affordable, and unsecured loans for your small or medium-sized business without risking your assets. Choose from flexible repayment options with small fixed monthly payments under Unsecured Business Finance.

Business Growth Loans
